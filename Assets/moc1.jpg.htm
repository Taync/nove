Moncler

Moncler was founded in 1952 in the mountain town of Monestier-de-Clermont, France, and today is headquartered in Italy. The brand's mountaineering heritage is still evident in its apparel, and its quintessential jackets have for decades been the choice of athletes, explorers and celebrities. Moncler's designs blend traditional and contemporary, and are at home in both the mountains and the city.
